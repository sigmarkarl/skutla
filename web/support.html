Support

Skutla is an independent open-source hobby project.

Getting help

If something isn't working or you have a question, please open an issue on GitHub: github.com/sigmarkarl/skutla/issues. All discussion happens there in the open. There is no email support, no helpdesk, and no operator behind a phone.

Common questions

Is there an account?
No. Your identity is a random ID generated on first launch and stored only on your device.

How do I keep my ratings and history when switching devices?
Tap the key icon, copy your backup string, paste it on the new device.

Why don't I see any drivers?
The app only shows drivers in your geohash cell (~5 km radius). Either nobody is online nearby, or location permission isn't granted.

Who handles the payment?
You and the driver, directly. Skutla doesn't touch the money — it just shows the driver's preferred payment method (Aur, PayPal, Venmo, WeChat, or cash) and lets you tap to open the right app.

How do I delete my data?
Clear the app's storage on your device, or uninstall. Skutla doesn't operate a server, so there's nothing to delete server-side.

Reporting safety concerns

Skutla is a peer-to-peer matching tool, not a transportation provider. It does not verify driver licenses, vehicle insurance, or background checks. Please use the rating system to flag bad experiences and exercise caution just like any encounter with a stranger. If something serious happens, contact local emergency services.
