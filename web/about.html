Skutla

Decentralized peer-to-peer ride matching. No middleman.

Drop a destination on the map. Drivers nearby submit price offers. Pick the one you like. No accounts, no commission, no central server taking a cut.

Try it in your browser Source on GitHub

What makes it different

  • Truly decentralized. No Skutla servers. Drivers and riders exchange messages directly via a public peer-to-peer broker.
  • You set the price. Each driver bids individually with a price they pick — passengers compare offers and accept their favorite.
  • Direct contact, direct payment. Driver shares phone, WhatsApp, or Messenger; rider pays via Aur, PayPal, Venmo, WeChat, or cash. The app never touches the money.
  • Local currency. Auto-detected from your location: Icelandic krona at home, dollars in the US, euros across the EU, and more.
  • Privacy by design. No accounts, no analytics, no tracking. Your data lives on your device.
  • Open source. MIT-licensed Flutter app, full source on GitHub.

How it works

  1. Open the app, allow location, pick a name and a role.
  2. Passenger: tap the map for your destination, tap Request ride.
  3. Drivers in your neighborhood see your request, submit a price.
  4. You pick a bid; the driver's car icon turns green on the map.
  5. Tap their phone, WhatsApp, or Messenger to coordinate the pickup.
  6. Pay them directly through the payment chip after the ride.
  7. Rate each other.
